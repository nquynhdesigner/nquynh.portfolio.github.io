This is the first website I designed. A website about baby clothes. Why did I choose this topic for myself? I really like fashion. Since I have children, the more I learn, the more the fashion world of children is equally interesting as adults. The baby's outfits, most are carefully tailored. The motifs are also carefully selected. If the world of adult fashion is diverse, rich from color to style, the world of children's fashion revolves around basic styles such as bodysuit, long pants. Although somewhat simple, it is really lovely.

That's why I chose pink and brown as the main color for my website design. Just fresh and warm. A casual and lovely color tone. I want moms to feel comfortable watching my website. I want to give them a simple experience so most of the icons and web page manipulation are easy to follow. From member registration to clicking your favorite costume.

What is special on this website?

This site has a very unique style of children's fashion. Simple outfits with sunken lace patterns and trendy brown. A gentle style, old style. If you are looking for color then this site is clearly not suitable. However, I still wish you could visit and enjoy the site and these lovely outfits. Maybe you will like it?
